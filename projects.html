Project 1: Interface Design for "Sahiba"

The "Sahiba" interface project exemplifies my passion for creating visually pleasing yet user-friendly designs. Drawing inspiration from the graceful and elegant nature of the name Sahiba, I crafted an interface that blends modern minimalism with subtle artistic flourishes. The design focuses on clarity and ease of navigation, utilizing a calm color palette and intuitive iconography to engage users effectively. This project highlights my ability to balance aesthetics with functionality, producing an interface that is both beautiful and practical. The layout adapts gracefully across devices, ensuring a seamless user experience whether on desktop or mobile.

Project 2: Instagram Graphics - "Stuff Happens, Chew Up The Stress"

In this Instagram graphic design project, I aimed to visually express the relatable sentiment "Stuff happens, chew up the stress" with a lively and playful approach. The artwork combines bold typography with vibrant colors and engaging illustrations that invite viewers to pause and reflect. Through this design, I explored the dynamic use of contrast and layering to capture attention on social media platforms. The graphic was tailored to fit Instagram’s format perfectly, ensuring maximum visibility and engagement. This project underscores my skills in creating compelling social media visuals that effectively communicate messages and connect emotionally with audiences.

Project 3: Advertising Brochure for Bose Headphones

This advertising brochure for Bose headphones showcases my capability in crafting elegant marketing materials that merge high-quality imagery with persuasive content. Project 4: Cookie Packaging Label Design
