Hello, Welcome to my portfolio

Hi, I’m Gurman Kahlon—a graphic designer who believes every design should feel as good as it looks. My work lives at the sweet spot between clean minimalism and subtle playfulness, where elegance meets personality. I love bringing ideas to life in a way that’s simple, intentional, and a little bit unexpected—whether it’s a brand identity, packaging, or a digital design. For me, design isn’t just about making something pretty; it’s about creating visuals that tell a story, spark curiosity, and leave a lasting impression.
