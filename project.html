Cookie Box Design

This project involved designing a modern and attractive cookie box that would appeal to a wide range of customers. The design integrates playful colors, clear typography, and a professional layout to ensure the packaging looks inviting and eye-catching on shelves. I focused on brand consistency, incorporating elements that reflect the warmth and freshness of homemade cookies. From the logo placement to ingredient highlights and eco-friendly packaging notes, the design enhances consumer engagement while maintaining practicality. The final layout was tested on mockups for presentation and usability.

Fruity Pop Can Design

For this project, I created a vibrant and energetic soda can design targeted toward younger audiences. Using a mix of fruity illustrations, bold color palettes, and dynamic text treatments, I ensured the brand identity resonated with fun and freshness. The main objective was to make the product stand out in a competitive market by emphasizing uniqueness and appeal. I explored different design iterations before finalizing the layout that combines clarity and playfulness. The can design aligns with industry standards and conveys the product's flavor and excitement effectively.

Bose Headphones Tri-Fold Brochure

This tri-fold brochure project was focused on designing a sleek and modern marketing material for Bose headphones. I adopted a minimalist approach to reflect the premium quality of the brand while using high-resolution images, clear headings, and concise content to communicate features effectively. The layout was structured to guide the reader through the experience of using Bose headphones — highlighting sound quality, comfort, and innovation. This print-ready brochure was designed to serve both informative and promotional purposes, and it balances style and function to resonate with audiophiles and casual listeners alike.
